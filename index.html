My Expericence

Dance

Indian classical dance has always fascinated me since childhood. This was indeed the triggering point for me to explore it as a career option as part of my project in grade 7. I met with various dances in Kerala including, Mrs.Gopika Varma, Mrs.Girija, Mrs. Mythili and Mrs. Deepa Rahul Easwar. These meetings included interviews, where I would ask them about their challenges, changes in lifestyle, etc they experienced, when taking dance as a career. These sessions helped me analyse and understand the potential difficulties an individual will face and the amount of dedication needed to become a successful Guru. I was able to conclude that, for me, dance was just a hobby and not a potential career path.
